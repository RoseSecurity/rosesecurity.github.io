# Services:

RoseSecurity Consulting is dedicated to improving the security posture of your company through engineering tailored and scalable solutions to meet your organization's needs.  We do this by offering security testing and vulnerability assessment services to identify key points of leverage that an adversary could exploit to cause maximum impact. After identifying key points of leverage, we develop a strategic and tactical plan of action to strengthen your network and drive continual improvement.

## Assumed Compromise Assessment:

Focused on the impact of a malicious insider from within your organization's network. This assessment will raise the bar on your internal security through identifying sensitive pivot points and unknowns.  

## Web Application Penetration Testing:

Comprehensive analysis of your organization's internal and external web applications to identify misconfigurations and vulnerabilities that could lead to exploitation. 

## External Network Penetration Testing:

Assessing the publicly-facing attack surface of your company, we perform a vulnerability scan of your assets and exploit discoveries to gain internal access to your organization's network.

## Phishing Assessment:

Deployment of a sophisticated phishing attack to gain a foothold into your corporate network. Leveraging this access, we explore the full potential of a successful compromise with unparalleled depth.
